---

## After Upgrading to Twilio (Recommended)

Twilio is an official WhatsApp service that removes the PC dependency.
Your bot will run fully in the cloud — no PC needed.

### If You Receive 500 Messages Per Month

| Service         | What It Does                              | Monthly Cost     |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------|------------------|
| Railway         | Keeps your bot's brain (n8n) running 24/7 | ~$5.00           |
| Twilio WhatsApp | Sends & receives WhatsApp messages        | ~$2.50 – $4.00   |
| Groq AI         | Understands customer messages             | Free             |
| Google Sheets   | Stores your freight rates                 | Free             |
| **Total**       |                                           | **~$7.50 – $9.00/month** |

### How Twilio Charges Work

- You pay **per message** — both messages received and replies sent count
- Rate: **$0.005 to $0.008 per message**
- 500 customer inquiries = roughly 1,000 messages (1 received + 1 reply each)
- Estimated Twilio bill: **$5.00 – $8.00** for 500 inquiries

> 💡 **Simple way to think about it:**
> Every customer inquiry costs you less than **1 cent** to handle automatically —
> no human operator needed.
